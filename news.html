new_releases Easter Bunny's Journey Tracker upgraded to v1.1
schedule January 30, 2025

The Easter Bunny's Journey Tracker just got an upgrade to v1.1, and we've added some cool new features! Here's what's new:


🕰️ Current local time display

Now, in the additional stats box, you'll see the current local time of wherever the Easter Bunny is right now. (✨ Fun fact: The globe emoji next to it now dynamically updates to match the sector of the Easter Bunny's current timezone. Pretty neat, right?)


🛰️ ISS stop on the map

Did you know the Easter Bunny stops at the International Space Station too? Well, now it's easier to spot! The ISS stop on the map has a special satellite icon 🛰️ so you can easily recognize it. As we get closer to Easter Eve, we'll also update the location to show the correct position of the space station!


📶 Offline tracking???

The biggest change—now the tracker works without an internet connection! That's right, even without Wi-Fi, you can still follow the Easter Bunny's journey. How? 🤔 Using advanced algorithms, the tracker predicts the Bunny's location based on his route and past places he visited. The experience is almost identical to the online version, though there are a couple of differences. Map tiles might not load properly sometimes, and the weather & Wikipedia overview features won't be available offline. But other than that, you can still keep track of the Bunny's adventure wherever you are!


So, check out the new features and enjoy the enhanced experience! 🐰🎉
